# My Journey into Tech

It started with a simple `print("Hello, World!")`. I remember sitting in my dorm room, staring at the terminal cursor blinking back at me.

## The Django Phase
My first love was **Django**. The batteries-included philosophy made me feel like I had superpowers. 

> "Coding is the closest thing we have to magic."

I built a simple todo list, then a blog, then an e-commerce backend. I loved how Python made complex logic feel readable and elegant.
